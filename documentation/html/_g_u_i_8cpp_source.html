173  * Some relevant refresh rates:
174  * 1-10 fps:
175  * - Indicates extremely low performance.

179  * - Point at which the human eye starts to perceive separate images as continuous.
180  * 24 fps:
181  * - Standard cinematic frame rate.
182  * 30 fps:
183  * - Half of NTSC refresh rate (approximately).
184  * - Became standard for some time.
185  * - Is now considered the acceptable minimum fps for some platforms.
186  * 50 fps:
187  * - PAL standard.
188  * 60 fps:
189  * - NTSC standard (approximately).
190  * - Modern standard for acceptable refresh rate.
191  * - Highest supported framerate on most non-overclocked consumer displays.

193  * - 3 x 24 fps.
